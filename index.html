About Me.

Hi! I’m Iffah Zaara, an Interior Design student who loves transforming everyday spaces into meaningful experiences. Design, for me, is more than just how a place looks — it’s about how it makes people feel. I enjoy blending aesthetics with functionality to create spaces that are comfortable, creative, and full of life.
My interests include exploring different design styles, experimenting with colors and textures, and finding inspiration in nature, art, and architecture. I’m passionate about learning new design concepts and improving my skills through every project I take on.
Whether it’s a cozy home interior, a lively café, or a calm workspace, I believe every design should tell a story and reflect the people who live or work within it. My goal is to continue growing as a designer who creates spaces that inspire and connect people.

Works.

01

COTTAGE.

The Cottage is designed to feel warm, natural, and peaceful. Soft green colors and wooden textures give the space a cozy and relaxing look. The design brings in lots of natural light and uses simple furniture to keep the space open and calm. Every corner is made to feel comfortable and welcoming, with small details that add charm and character. The mix of modern and cottage-style elements creates a perfect balance between freshness and homeliness. It’s a space that feels close to nature and full of quiet beauty.

02

LIVING ROOM.

This is a Cornerless Living Room which is designed to feel open and free, without sharp edges or tight spaces. Soft curves and flowing lines make the area look spacious and calm. Natural colors and smooth textures add warmth, while the open layout creates easy movement and connection. The space feels modern, comfortable, and welcoming from every angle.

03

BEDROOM.

The Serene Bedroom is designed with soft beige shades and natural elements to create a calm and peaceful space. Light tones and gentle textures bring warmth and comfort, while eco-friendly materials keep the room close to nature. The design feels simple, airy, and relaxing — a perfect place to unwind and rest.

04

CLINIC.

This is a Kids-Friendly Clinic designed to feel bright, gentle, and welcoming. Soft colors create a space where children feel safe and comfortable. The layout is open and calming, with cozy seating and natural light that adds warmth. The design blends care and creativity, making the clinic both cheerful and soothing.

05

DINING.

The Dining & Bar Space is designed to bring people together in a warm and stylish setting. Soft lighting and natural textures create a cozy atmosphere, while the bar station adds a modern and lively touch. The space balances comfort and elegance, making it perfect for both casual meals and relaxed gatherings.

06

KITCHEN.

The Modern Kitchen features rich dark blue shades paired with warm wooden tones, creating a bold yet cozy atmosphere. The color adds depth and character, while natural textures keep the space balanced and welcoming. Clean lines and smart storage make it both stylish and practical, bringing together function and comfort in a modern way.
